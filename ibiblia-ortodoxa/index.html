iBiblia Ortodoxa

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the iOS application iBiblia Ortodoxa handles user data.

1. Data Collection

iBiblia Ortodoxa does not collect personal data, does not require account creation, and does not send personal information to external servers.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores a limited set of reading preferences locally on your device to support the reading experience. This may include:

  • text size preference
  • reading theme preference
  • last opened Bible book order

This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to the developer or third parties.

3. Analytics and Tracking

iBiblia Ortodoxa does not use analytics services, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or third-party profiling tools.

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party sign-in, advertising, analytics, cloud sync, or remote data collection services in its current version.

5. Children’s Privacy

iBiblia Ortodoxa does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

6. Your Choices

Because the app stores preferences locally on your device, you may remove this data at any time by deleting the app from your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future if the app’s features or data practices change. Any updates will be reflected on this page.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through: https://github.com/taruxoph
